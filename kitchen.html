Bacon Wrapped Dates

I made these delicious bite-sized appetizers for my mom's birthday and for Mother's Day. Sweet pitted dates stuffed with your cheese of choice (I used Jarlsberg) and fresh rosemary, wrapped in mouthwatering bacon, and drizzled with a sweet and spicy honey-cayenne glaze.

Bursting Blueberry Tarts

I also made this for my mom's birthday, and it's the perfect combination of sweet and tart, especially with lots of lemon. I didn't have thyme, so I omitted that, but went ham on the lemon zest! I would recommend if you like tart flavors.
