Books

The Art of Happiness

By Dalai Lama and Dr. Howard Cutler

The book explores training the human outlook that alters perception. The concepts that the purpose of life is happiness, that happiness is determined more by the state of one’s mind than by one’s external conditions, circumstances, or events— at least once one’s basic survival needs are met and that happiness can be achieved through the systematic training of our hearts and minds.

Leaving Microsoft to Change the World

By John Wood

In 1998, John Wood was a rising executive at Microsoft when he took a vacation that changed his life. What started as a trekking holiday in Nepal became a spiritual journey and then a mission: to change the world one book and one child at a time by setting up libraries in the developing world.

A New Earth

By Eckhart Tolle

A “self-improvement book” that encourages its readers to live their lives in each present moment and to create happiness for themselves without emphasizing material possessions. Tolle’s intent is to change the way human beings think, and he envisions a world population is increasingly humble, enlightened, and pure.
